    &= \frac{1}{2r_1^2} \frac{\partial}{\partial r_1} \left[ r_1^2-\alpha \right] \\
    &= \frac{1}{2r_1^2} \Big( -2r_1\alpha \Big) \\
    &= -\frac{\alpha}{r_1}
\end{align*}
